Early Menopause Mental Health Support

Low Self-Esteem

Whether you're experiencing menopause before 45 or premature menopause before 40, the impact on your mental health is real, and so is the support available to you.

Mood Changes

Hormonal shifts can lead to irritability, sadness, anxiety, or emotional overwhelm. You are not alone, and you deserve support.

Brain Fog

Difficulty concentrating, memory lapses, and mental fatigue are common and can deeply affect your day-to-day life.

Navigating early menopause doesn't have to feel isolating.

Share With Others

Speaking with friends, family or a therapist can help you process emotions and feel supported.

Healthy Routines

Regular sleep, gentle movement, nutritious food, and hydration can stabilise mood and energy.

Hormonal Options

Discuss the possibility of HRT, which can support both physical and emotional symptoms caused by hormonal changes.

There are tools, treatments, and communities there when things get difficult.
