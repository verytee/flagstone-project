The Early Menopause can affect Your Mental Health

Low Self-Esteem

Whether you're experiencing menopause before 45 or premature menopause before 40, the impact on your mental health is real, and so is the support available to you.

Mood Changes

Hormonal shifts can lead to irritability, sadness, anxiety, or emotional overwhelm. You are not alone in this experience, and support is available to help you navigate these changes.

Brain Fog

Difficulty concentrating, memory lapses, and mental fatigue are common symptoms that impact on mental health.

Navigating early menopause doesn't have to feel isolating.
